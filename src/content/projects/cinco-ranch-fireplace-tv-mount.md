## Project overview

This Cinco Ranch living room needed a 75-inch display mounted above a custom stone fireplace — level, secure, and finished so the wires did not distract from the formal room. Stone and tile mounts require masonry bits and heavy-duty anchors; drywall-only approaches fail here.

## Scope of work

- Located a safe mounting position centered on the fireplace
- Anchored a heavy-duty mount into the stone assembly
- Concealed cabling as cleanly as the wall conditions allowed
- Leveled, tested, and cleaned the work area

## Why it matters in Cinco Ranch

Homes in [Cinco Ranch](/service-areas/katy/cinco-ranch-handyman/) often mix traditional formal living rooms with modern entertainment needs. A fireplace mount has to respect the masonry and the room's proportions — especially in established 77450 sections where finishes are mature and visible mistakes are expensive.
