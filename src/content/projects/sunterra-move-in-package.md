## Project overview

A new Sunterra homeowner wanted the house livable before the moving truck emptied — not a month of ladders and half-finished rooms. We executed a coordinated move-in package across entertainment, airflow, and garage organization.

## Scope of work

- Mounted three TVs on fresh drywall and fireplace surfaces ([TV mounting](/services/tv-mounting/))
- Installed five ceiling fans on builder pre-wires ([ceiling fan installation](/services/ceiling-fan-installation/))
- Added overhead garage storage for boxes and seasonal items
- Protected new floors and finishes throughout
