## What professional mounting includes

Stud location or proper anchoring for the wall type, correct bracket selection (fixed, tilt, or full-motion), level placement, and wire concealment options where the wall and codes allow. We confirm TV size and weight against the mount rating before hardware goes into the wall. If new in-wall power is required, we help coordinate licensed electrical support rather than improvising unsafe shortcuts.

On install day we protect floors, mark carefully, cut only what is necessary for concealment, hang and level the display, and test tilt or articulation so you can use the TV immediately. Packaging and debris leave with us — your living room should look finished, not like a job site.
