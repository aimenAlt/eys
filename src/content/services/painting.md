## What careful interior painting includes

Good painting is mostly preparation: masking, protecting floors, cutting clean edges, and choosing the right sheen for kitchens, baths, and hallways. We treat your home like a finished living space — shoe covers, drop cloths, and a full cleanup before we consider the work done. If walls need patches first, we will say so up front. Combining paint with drywall in one coordinated visit saves you from coordinating two trades and living with mismatched patches.

Cabinet painting is quoted separately when scope warrants it; send photos so we can recommend the right approach rather than forcing a wall-paint process onto cabinet work.
