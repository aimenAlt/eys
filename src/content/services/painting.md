Interior painting is one of the fastest ways to make a West Houston home feel cared for — whether you are refreshing builder-beige walls in a new Fulshear home or modernizing a 1990s Katy living room that still shows original color choices.

## Who this service is for

We paint rooms, accent walls, and repair areas for homeowners who want crisp lines and protected floors without managing a large painting crew. Typical projects include:

- Accent walls and color updates in [Elyson](/service-areas/katy/elyson-handyman/) and [Aliana](/service-areas/richmond/aliana-handyman/)
- Touch-ups after [drywall repair](/services/drywall-repair/)
- Pre-sale refreshes in established [Cinco Ranch](/service-areas/katy/cinco-ranch-handyman/) homes

## What careful interior painting includes

Good painting is mostly preparation: masking, protecting floors, cutting clean edges, and choosing the right sheen for kitchens, baths, and hallways. We treat your home like a finished living space, not a job site — shoe covers, drop cloths, and a full cleanup before we consider the work done.

If walls need patches first, we will say so up front. Combining paint with drywall in one coordinated visit saves you from coordinating two trades and living with mismatched patches.

## Local context

New-construction communities in Fulshear and Sunterra often need personality on top of builder white. Maturing Katy homes more often need color modernization and repair blending after years of family life. Richmond's mix — agri-hood farmhouse aesthetics in Harvest Green, porch living in Veranda — benefits from intentional accent choices that match how the home is used.
