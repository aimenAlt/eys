## What we build and install

Custom benches, floating shelves, trim packages, accent walls, and many built-in projects that improve storage and architecture. We discuss materials, stain or paint finish, and how the piece meets existing baseboards and casing before cutting. Large millwork packages may involve specialty partners; we stay transparent about scope so you are never surprised mid-project.

Process typically moves from inspiration photos and rough measurements, to a free estimate with material notes, then on-site build or install with floor protection and a thorough cleanup. Paint-ready surfaces can be coordinated with our [painting](/services/painting/) service when you want color and carpentry finished together. Closet and mudroom systems are popular because they solve daily clutter instead of simply decorating an empty wall.
