Custom carpentry is how production homes become personal — mudroom benches, built-ins, trim upgrades, media walls, and finish woodwork that looks like it belonged in the plan from day one. Elevate Your Space brings that craft to Katy and West Houston with clean shops-in-the-home habits and respect for existing finishes.

## Who this is for

- Families needing drop-zones and mudrooms in Elyson, Bridgeland, and Cross Creek Ranch
- Homeowners wanting shiplap, board-and-batten, or feature walls in Harvest Green and similar communities
- Luxury homes in Cane Island that need trim and built-ins matching high-end finishes
- Anyone tired of flat-pack “almost” solutions that never quite fit the wall

## What we build and install

Custom benches, floating shelves, trim packages, accent walls, and many built-in projects that improve storage and architecture. We discuss materials, stain or paint finish, and how the piece meets existing baseboards and casing before cutting. Large millwork packages may involve specialty partners; we stay transparent about scope.

## Local examples

Active trail communities need gear storage and mudrooms that survive real life. Agri-hood aesthetics favor farmhouse trim details. Established Cinco Ranch homes often need carpentry that respects traditional suburban architecture rather than fighting it.
