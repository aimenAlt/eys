- Katy homeowners modernizing 1990s–2000s kitchens without a six-month remodel

## What we typically deliver

[Cabinet installation](/services/cabinet-installation/) and hardware upgrades, select cabinet adjustments, fixture coordination, minor carpentry, backsplash and finish sequencing, and the trim details that make a kitchen feel intentional.
