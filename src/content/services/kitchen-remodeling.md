Kitchen remodeling at Elevate Your Space usually means high-impact improvements — hardware, cabinet adjustments, finishing details, and coordinated punch lists — not necessarily a full gut renovation. That is the gap many West Houston homeowners actually need filled.

## Who this is for

- Katy homeowners modernizing 1990s–2000s kitchens without a six-month remodel
- New-build owners upgrading builder-grade cabinets and lighting after closing
- Richmond and Fulshear families who want a kitchen that works harder for weeknight cooking

## What we typically deliver

Cabinet hardware installation, select cabinet installs and adjustments, fixture coordination, minor carpentry, and the finishing work that makes a kitchen feel intentional. When plumbing, gas, or major electrical changes are required, we help coordinate licensed trades instead of pretending a handyman visit covers everything.
