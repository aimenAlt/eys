## What we handle

Interior doors, closet doors, hardware refreshes, strike-plate adjustments, hinge replacements, weatherstripping touch-ups, and many exterior door installations or repairs fall in our sweet spot. We pay attention to casing and finish so the work matches the home rather than looking like a quick swap. When a door project requires specialized millwork or structural reframing beyond handyman scope, we say so before you spend money.

On a typical visit we diagnose swing and latch issues, adjust or re-hang as needed, install new pre-hung units when you have purchased replacements, and test operation before we leave. Photos from both sides of the door, plus notes about sticking or hardware problems, help us prepare the right tools and hardware ahead of time.
