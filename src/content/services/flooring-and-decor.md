New floors and décor only look complete when the edges, transitions, and mounting details are done right. Elevate Your Space helps West Houston homeowners finish flooring-adjacent carpentry and décor installs so rooms feel intentional instead of almost-done.

## Who this is for

- Homeowners after a flooring delivery who still need transitions, trim, or adjustments
- Families hanging mirrors, shelves, and décor that need solid anchoring
- Move-in clients combining décor installs with [curtain installation](/services/curtain-installation/) and [TV mounting](/services/tv-mounting/)

## What we handle

Flooring-adjacent finish work, décor mounting, and the carpentry details that frame a room. We are not a full flooring replacement company for every material and moisture scenario — we will tell you when a specialty flooring crew is the right lead and where our finishing work adds the most value.

## Local context

Established Katy homes often need careful trim work around older casings when floors change. New Fulshear and Sunterra homes need décor and privacy installs quickly after furniture arrives. Richmond communities mix farmhouse décor (Harvest Green) with resort-style Aliana living — mounting and finish details should match that character.
