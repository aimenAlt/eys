## Who this is for

- Homeowners replacing dated lighting in 1990s–2000s Katy homes
- New-build buyers installing fixtures and fans after closing
- Anyone hanging heavier chandeliers or outdoor-rated fixtures who wants them done safely
- Families adding dimmers, smart switches, or outlet upgrades on existing circuits

## What we commonly do

Light fixture swaps, [ceiling fan installation](/services/ceiling-fan-installation/), outlet and switch replacements, and related mounting that improves comfort and appearance. We assess existing boxes and mounting conditions before we start. New circuits, service upgrades, and work requiring a licensed electrician are scoped honestly with coordination support — we do not treat panel or permit work as a casual add-on.

On site we shut power down at the correct breaker, verify the circuit is dead, install fixtures level and secure, and restore covers cleanly. High ceilings and foyer installs may need extra staging; we plan access so the finished chandelier or pendant hangs correctly on the first try.
