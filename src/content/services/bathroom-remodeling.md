Not every bathroom project needs a full gut remodel. Elevate Your Space helps Katy and West Houston homeowners upgrade baths with vanity installs, fixture swaps, hardware refreshes, and punch-list remodeling that improves daily use without weeks of disruption.

## Who this service is for

- Families in maturing Katy homes updating 1990s–2000s baths
- New-build owners who want better fixtures and storage after closing
- Sellers preparing baths for listing photos and showings
- Richmond and Fulshear homeowners who need practical upgrades, not a luxury gut job

## What bathroom upgrades typically include

Vanity installation, mirror mounting, faucet and fixture swaps, hardware updates, and related carpentry that makes the room feel finished. We scope carefully from photos and will tell you when plumbing or waterproofing work needs a licensed trade partner — we help coordinate that support rather than overselling handyman scope.

## Local examples

Cinco Ranch and early Katy MPC baths often need vanity and hardware modernization. Sunterra and Jordan Ranch buyers more often want finishing touches on brand-new baths. Harvest Green and Veranda projects sometimes lean into farmhouse or porch-living aesthetics with fixtures and trim that match the rest of the home.
