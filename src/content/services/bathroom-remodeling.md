## What bathroom upgrades typically include

Vanity installation, mirror mounting, faucet and fixture swaps, hardware updates, towel bars and accessories, and related carpentry that makes the room feel finished. We scope carefully from photos and a short walkthrough of goals. When plumbing rough-in, waterproofing, or major electrical changes sit outside handyman scope, we say so early and help coordinate licensed trade partners rather than overselling what a single visit can cover.

A typical project starts with photos and a priority list, moves into a clear free estimate and sequence of work, then proceeds with protected floors, tidy staging, and a clean handoff. Smaller refreshes — vanity swaps, hardware, and fixture updates — often fit one coordinated visit. Larger remodels are phased so you always know what happens next.
